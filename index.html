Welcome :3

Happy to have you here! My name is Claire Allen, and I am a writer and researcher spending most of my time scrolling towards a digital-subcultural underground under the guise of fieldwork. This developing practice subsists through intra- and extra-institutional study, lurking, and attempting to document information via ephemera passing through the digital commons.

In 2025, I completed my HBA at the John H. Daniels Faculty of Architecture, Landscape, and Design at the University of Toronto, where I focused my work in critical practices relating to popular visual culture and visual rhetoric. My undergraduate thesis researched contemporary girlblogging subcultures on Instagram, arguing for their placement within a canon of looking shaped by the male gaze and succeeding such fine artistic media as painting, photography, and film. That, and other scholarly projects of mine, can be found here. Other writings of mine can be found here.

Outside of these more serious endeavours I also find myself a collector of cultural detritus, especially in digital spaces. This collection can be found here.
